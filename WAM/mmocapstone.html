WAM (Wearable Art Music)

This is a capstone project that utilizes MIDI controller manipulation and music/movement manipulation. It is an outfit that can be worn on the wrists and legs that hold small cordless devices that then connect to a software program. When an individual moves, these different sections can manipulate different parts and sounds from a sound patch.

Article in regards to the capstone showcase: https://news.asu.edu/20240322-arts-humanities-and-education-massive-unveiling-creativity

These are the MIDI controllers that were utilized for the project in both prototype and final stages. The orange one is called M5StickC and has the capabilities of bluetooth to allow for the cordless movement control.
